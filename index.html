e-Commerce Sales Data

Key findings from our dataset analysis and visualization revealed that the average order processing time
stands at approximately 5 days. Shipping preferences indicate that 60% of orders are shipped standard and 51% first class, with an average shipping cost of $7 per order. Fashion emerged as the top-selling category, constituting 67% of total sales and generating $2.5m in profits, followed by home and furniture at 20%, yielding $587k profit Consumers contribute the most to sales at 51%, followed by corporate customers (30%) and home offices (18%). Geographically, the Central region leads with 22% of sales, trailed by the South (13%), EMEA (10%), and North (9%). These insights offer valuable opportunities for optimizing operations and driving profitability This analysis was performed using Microsoft Excel.

Using Power BI

In my analysis using Power BI, I found several key insights in the dataset. The most common property type is landed property accounting for 36% of sales, followed by commercial and residential properties at 32% each.
